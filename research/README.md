# Research

Workspace research outputs: investigations, audits, AI session notes, experiments, and design assumptions.

## Subtrees

| Folder | Contents |
|---|---|
| [investigations/](investigations/) | Code/data investigations, audits, AI session transcripts, workspace history |
| [design_assumptions/](design_assumptions/) | Documented assumptions baked into the design |
| [experiments/](experiments/) | Exploratory experiment notes |

## Conventions

- Each investigation is timestamped and self-contained.
- Audit logs and reorganization history are **append-only** — older entries are not edited even when paths change. See [investigations/workspace_history/](investigations/workspace_history/).
